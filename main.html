Plot Summary

Dom Cobb is a skilled thief who specializes in extraction - stealing secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. His rare ability has made him a coveted player in the treacherous new world of corporate espionage, but it has also made him an international fugitive and cost him everything he has ever loved. Now Cobb is being offered a chance at redemption through one last job that could give him his life back, but only if he can accomplish the seemingly impossible: inception. Instead of stealing an idea, he must plant one deep within someone's mind, navigating through multiple layers of dreams within dreams.

Cast & Actors

The stellar ensemble cast brings Christopher Nolan's complex narrative to life with exceptional performances.

Leonardo DiCaprio

Role: Dom Cobb

The skilled extractor and leader of the inception team, haunted by memories of his deceased wife Mal.

Marion Cotillard

Role: Mal Cobb

Cobb's deceased wife who appears as a projection in his dreams, representing his guilt and regret.

Tom Hardy

Role: Eames

A charismatic forger who can impersonate others within dreams, bringing wit and charm to the team.

Ellen Page

Role: Ariadne

The brilliant architecture student who designs the dream worlds and serves as the team's anchor to reality.
